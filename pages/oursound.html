Some of the things they're saying about us!

Experience Motown treasures from artists such as Stevie Wonder, the funky explosive intensity of James Brown, and the superb melodic classics from Stax label artists such as Wilson Picket, Sam and Dave and the legendary Otis Redding. If you liked the Blues Brothers, if you liked the Commitments you’ll love Soul Power. Soul Powers’ impressive CV includes working in music venues, clubs, weddings, parties, festivals, conferences, corporate entertainment and all kind of events both in the UK and abroad. The basic line up consists of Vocals, Guitar, Keys, Bass, Drums, Trumpet, Trombone and Sax. The band can be augmented with Female lead vocals and or female backing vocals and also a larger brass section including the authentic sound of a baritone sax.
